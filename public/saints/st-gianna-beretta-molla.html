St. Gianna Beretta Molla

Feast Day: April 28
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

St. Gianna was a working mother and a pediatrician. During her fourth pregnancy, she developed a fibroma on her uterus. She refused an abortion or a hysterectomy that would have killed the child, opting for a surgery that carried high risk for her. She died a week after giving birth to a healthy daughter.

Did you know? Her husband and four children were present at her canonization in 2004.
