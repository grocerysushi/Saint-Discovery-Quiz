Biography

St. Paul the Simple was a hermit who embraced a life of solitude and prayer in the Egyptian desert. Renowned for his innocence and genuine faith, he became a model of simplicity in a world often filled with complexity. His life served as an inspiration to many, showcasing the profound richness found in a humble existence devoted to God.

Did you know? Despite his simplicity, St. Paul had a remarkable ability to discern the hearts of those who visited him.
