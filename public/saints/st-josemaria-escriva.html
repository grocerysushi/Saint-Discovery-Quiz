Biography

St. Josemaria Escriva was a Spanish priest who dedicated his life to encouraging people to find God in their daily activities. He founded Opus Dei in 1928, emphasizing the importance of holiness in ordinary life. His teachings inspire millions to integrate their faith into their work and family lives.

Did you know? St. Josemaria Escriva loved to play with words and was known for his humorous quips, often making light of serious situations.
