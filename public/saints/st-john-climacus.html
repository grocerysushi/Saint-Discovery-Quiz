Biography

St. John Climacus, a prominent 7th-century monk, dedicated his life to the pursuit of spiritual perfection. He is best known for his writings that provide insight into the ascetic life and the path to holiness. His teachings continue to inspire and guide believers in their spiritual journeys today.

Did you know? St. John Climacus is revered in both the Eastern Orthodox and Roman Catholic traditions, illustrating his broad impact on Christian spirituality.

Patron Saint Of

Ladder climbers, monastics
