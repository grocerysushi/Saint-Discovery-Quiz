St. Bruno

Feast Day: October 6
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

St. Bruno was a profound spiritual figure who established the Carthusian monastic order in the early 11th century. He sought a deeper relationship with God through radical solitude and ascetic practices, advocating for a quiet contemplative life. His legacy endures as a testament to the power of silence and prayer in the pursuit of holiness.

Did you know? St. Bruno has been claimed to have had a vision of the Holy Trinity, which inspired him to devote his life to the Carthusian ideals.

Patron Saint Of
