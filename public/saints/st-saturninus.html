St. Saturninus

Biography

St. Saturninus was a prominent early Christian leader in Toulouse, known for his unwavering faith during a time of great persecution. According to tradition, he was martyred for refusing to renounce Christianity, highlighting his dedication to God. His legacy has continued to inspire many believers throughout the centuries.

Did you know? St. Saturninus is often depicted with a bull, symbolizing his martyrdom, as he was said to have been tied to one and dragged to his death.
