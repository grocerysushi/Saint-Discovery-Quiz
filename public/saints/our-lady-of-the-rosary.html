Biography

Our Lady of the Rosary dates back to the 13th century, reflecting the importance of the rosary in Catholic devotion. She is seen as a powerful intercessor and has inspired countless faithful to seek her guidance through prayer. The feast emphasizes the role of Mary in the church and the spiritual strength of the rosary.

Did you know? The feast was established in 1571 to commemorate the victory of Christian forces at the Battle of Lepanto, which was attributed to the intercession of the Virgin Mary.
