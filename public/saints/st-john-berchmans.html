St. John Berchmans

Biography

St. John Berchmans was a Belgian Jesuit who dedicated his short life to the service of God and his fellow man. He is remembered for his piety and dedication to learning, earning him the esteem of many during his lifetime. His example continues to inspire young people, especially students and altar servers, to live with integrity and devotion.

Did you know? Despite his young age at the time of his death, John was noted for his remarkable calmness and discipline.
