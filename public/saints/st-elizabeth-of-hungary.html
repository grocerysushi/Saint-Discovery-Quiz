St. Elizabeth of Hungary

Feast Day: November 17
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Elizabeth was a princess who married the Landgrave of Thuringia. She dedicated herself to works of charity, spending her dowry on a hospital where she personally served the sick. After her husband's death, she joined the Third Order of St. Francis.

Did you know? The 'Miracle of the Roses' tells of how her husband once met her while she was secretly carrying bread to the poor, and when he asked to see what was in her apron, it was filled with beautiful roses instead of bread.
