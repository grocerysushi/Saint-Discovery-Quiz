Biography

St. Peregrine Laziosi was a 14th-century Italian saint known for his deep faith and miraculous cures. After suffering from cancer himself, he experienced a profound healing through prayer and devotion, which inspired many to seek his intercession. He became a beloved figure, advocating for the sick and marginalized in society.

Did you know? St. Peregrine's cancerous leg was said to have been miraculously restored, leaving no trace of illness.
