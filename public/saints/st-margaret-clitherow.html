St. Margaret Clitherow

Feast Day: March 25
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

St. Margaret Clitherow was born in England and became a devoted wife and mother who bravely hid priests to protect them from persecution. Her actions led to her arrest and trial, where she refused to betray her faith, ultimately sacrificing her life for her beliefs. Recognized as a martyr for her courage, she inspires many to stand firm in their convictions.

Did you know? Margaret Clitherow was executed by pressing, a unique form of martyrdom where heavy weights were placed on her body, yet she remained steadfast in her faith until the end.
