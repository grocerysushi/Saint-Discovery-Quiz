Biography

St. Apollonia was a Christian martyr from Egypt who faced horrific torture, notably having her teeth pulled out. She remained steadfast in her faith during her trials, inspiring countless believers with her unwavering courage. Her memory is honored by those who seek comfort and healing from tooth-related issues.

Did you know? St. Apollonia is often invoked by those suffering from dental pain, and her iconography frequently features a pair of pliers or a tooth.

Patron Saint Of

Dentists, toothache
