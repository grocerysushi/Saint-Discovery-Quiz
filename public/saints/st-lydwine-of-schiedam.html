St. Lydwine of Schiedam

Feast Day: July 16
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

St. Lydwine was a Dutch mystic who experienced a series of debilitating illnesses from a young age. Despite her intense suffering, she found solace in her faith and had numerous visions, serving as a spiritual guide for her community. Her profound journey and dedication led to her being revered as a saint and the patroness of the sick.

Did you know? Lydwine was said to have been gifted with the ability to bilocate, appearing to others while remaining at her place of confinement.

Patron Saint Of
