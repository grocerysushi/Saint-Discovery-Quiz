Biography

St. Gemma Galgani was born in Italy and devoted her life to prayer and the deep love of Jesus Christ. Known for her mystical experiences, including the stigmata, she endured great suffering yet remained a beacon of faith. Her legacy continues to inspire countless individuals seeking solace and guidance in their spiritual journeys.

Did you know? Despite her short life, St. Gemma was known for writing numerous letters that conveyed her deep spiritual insights and experiences with God.

Patron Saint Of

Students, pharmacists, those suffering back injuries, loss of parents
