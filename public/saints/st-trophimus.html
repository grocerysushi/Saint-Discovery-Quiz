Biography

St. Trophimus is known as a companion of St. Paul, having served as a supporter in his ministry during the early Christian church. A devoted and tireless worker in spreading the Gospel, he was eventually martyred for his faith, exemplifying courage and conviction. His unwavering dedication to Christ and his compassion for the marginalized have made him a revered figure in the Catholic Church.

Did you know? Did you know? St. Trophimus was believed to have been one of the first converts in Asia Minor, playing a crucial role in establishing the Christian faith in that region.
