Biography

St. Veronica is remembered for her act of kindness towards Jesus during His Passion. Her bravery and devotion led her to defy the crowd to offer Him comfort in His time of suffering. This encounter not only solidified her place in Christian tradition but also established her as a symbol of compassion and empathy.

Did you know? The cloth that St. Veronica used to wipe Jesus' face is said to bear His actual image, which is considered a holy relic in Christian tradition.
