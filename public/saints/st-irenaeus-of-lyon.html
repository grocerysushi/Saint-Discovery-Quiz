Biography

St. Irenaeus of Lyon was a prominent early Christian theologian and bishop who played a crucial role in the development of Christian doctrine. He is primarily remembered for his work 'Against Heresies,' where he systematically refuted Gnostic teachings and emphasized the importance of apostolic tradition. His commitment to the truth of faith and the unity of the Church has left a lasting legacy in Christianity.

Did you know? Irenaeus is one of the earliest proponents of the idea that the four Gospels were established as a definitive standard for the Church, contrasting with other texts that surfaced in early Christianity.
