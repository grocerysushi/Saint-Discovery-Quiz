Biography

St. Elizabeth of Portugal was a queen renowned for her peacebuilding efforts during a time of family conflicts and war. She dedicated her life to helping the needy and was known to perform miracles, including healing the sick. Her legacy of charity and diplomacy continues to inspire people to seek peace in their own lives.

Did you know? St. Elizabeth was said to have had a special bond with her husband, King Dinis, which was so strong that it inspired many stories of their legendary devotion to each other.
