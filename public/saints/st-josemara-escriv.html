Biography

St. Josemaría Escrivá was a Spanish priest who dedicated his life to encouraging people to pursue holiness through their daily work. He founded Opus Dei in 1928, promoting the idea that everyone is called to be a saint, regardless of their profession. His teachings continue to inspire countless individuals worldwide to integrate their faith into every aspect of their lives.

Did you know? St. Josemaría Escrivá was beatified by Pope John Paul II in 1992 and canonized in 2002, making him one of the few saints recognized in such a relatively short time after their death.
