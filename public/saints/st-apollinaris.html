Biography

St. Apollinaris was one of the early bishops of Ravenna who faced persecution for his steadfast faith. He is believed to have been martyred for his efforts to evangelize the region, and his legacy continues to influence the Church today. His dedication to Christ and unwavering spirit inspires many to remain steadfast in their faith despite challenges.

Did you know? St. Apollinaris is often depicted with a palm branch, symbolizing his martyrdom.

Patron Saint Of

bishops, caretakers, epilepsy, martyrs, and the city of Ravenna
