Biography

Maria suffered from Parkinson's disease which left her completely paralyzed and in constant pain. Despite this, she was transformed into a spiritual elder who could read souls. People lined up outside her house in Gatchina to receive her counsel. She was finally arrested and died in a prison hospital.
