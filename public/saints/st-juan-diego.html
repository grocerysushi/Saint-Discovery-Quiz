St. Juan Diego

Feast Day: December 9
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Juan Diego was a Chichimeca peasant when the Virgin Mary appeared to him on Tepeyac Hill in 1531. She asked for a church to be built, and when the bishop requested a sign, the Virgin filled Juan Diego's cloak with out-of-season roses and left her miraculous image on the fabric.

Did you know? His 'tilma' (cloak) made of cactus fiber survives today in perfect condition in Mexico City, defying all scientific explanation for its durability and the nature of the image.
