St. Hubert

Biography

St. Hubert was a nobleman from Toulouse who experienced a profound conversion to Christianity during a hunting expedition. Following his transformation, he became a bishop and devoted his life to preaching and serving the community. His legacy as the patron saint of hunters and wildlife reflects his deep connection to nature and the divine.

Did you know? St. Hubert is often depicted with a stag, which symbolizes his miraculous encounter and conversion experience.

Patron Saint Of

hunters, dogs, forest workers, metalworkers, archers
