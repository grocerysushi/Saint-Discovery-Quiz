Bl. Isidore Bakanja

Feast Day: August 15
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Blessed Isidore Bakanja was a devoted layman in the Congo who encountered severe persecution for his Christian beliefs. He is best known for his resolve to wear the scapular and spread the teachings of Christ despite the threats against him. His martyrdom symbolizes the courage and dedication of Christians enduring oppression, inspiring many in their faith journeys.

Did you know? Isidore's commitment to forgiveness even in the face of violence transformed his persecutor's heart after his death.
