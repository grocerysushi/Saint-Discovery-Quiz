Biography

St. Lazarus, a close friend of Jesus, is famously remembered for his miraculous resurrection from the dead after four days. His story, found in the Gospel of John, illustrates the power of faith and the promise of eternal life. After his resurrection, he became a prominent figure in the early Christian community, symbolizing hope and renewal.

Did you know? Lazarus is sometimes considered the first example of a person raised from death to life in Christian theology.

Patron Saint Of

sick people, lepers, cancers, friends of the poor
