St. Charles of Mount Argus

Feast Day: January 18
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Born in the Netherlands, St. Charles worked tirelessly in his priestly ministry, especially in the care of the sick. He was known for his kindness and his ability to comfort those suffering from various ailments. His life exemplified a profound commitment to God and to serving others through love and hope.

Did you know? St. Charles was canonized in 2007, highlighting his impact in his local community and beyond.

Patron Saint Of
