Feast Day: August 29

Biography

St. Euphrasia Eluvathingal was an Indian Carmelite nun of the Syro-Malabar Church known for her deep mystical prayer life. She spent hours in Eucharistic adoration daily and was sought out by people from all walks of life for her spiritual counsel. She was canonized by Pope Francis in 2014.

Did you know? She was called 'Evuprasiamma' (Mother Euphrasia) by locals who lined up outside her convent seeking her prayers and counsel.
