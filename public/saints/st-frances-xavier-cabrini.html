St. Frances Xavier Cabrini

Feast Day: November 13
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

St. Frances Xavier Cabrini, known as Mother Cabrini, dedicated her life to serving Italian immigrants in the United States. She founded the Missionary Sisters of the Sacred Heart, establishing numerous schools, hospitals, and orphanages throughout the country. Her unwavering spirit and commitment to her mission earned her the title of the patron saint of immigrants.

Did you know? She was an asthmatic child and faced many health challenges but went on to travel extensively to serve her community.
