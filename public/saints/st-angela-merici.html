Biography

St. Angela Merici was a visionary educator who dedicated her life to improving the spiritual and educational landscape of her time. As a strong advocate for women's education, she established the Ursuline order in 1535, emphasizing the importance of teaching girls. Her legacy continues to inspire countless educators and students today.

Did you know? St. Angela Merici was known for her mystical experiences and had a deep commitment to prayer and spiritual reflection.
