St. Padre Pio

Feast Day: September 23

Biography

St. Padre Pio, born Francesco Forgione, became a Capuchin friar and is best known for bearing the stigmata for over 50 years. He was a prolific confessor, often spending hours each day in the sacrament of reconciliation, profoundly impacting countless lives. His mysticism and extraordinary gifts, including bilocation, have made him a beloved figure in the Catholic Church.

Did you know? St. Padre Pio was known to have the ability to bilocate, being seen in two places at once during his lifetime.

Patron Saint Of
