St. Martha

Feast Day: July 29
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

St. Martha is known for her unwavering faith and commitment to serving Jesus and her family. As the sister of Mary and Lazarus, she welcomed Christ into her home and displayed deep devotion amidst her busy life. Her character exemplifies the value of service and hospitality in the Christian faith.

Did you know? St. Martha is often depicted with a broom or keys, symbolizing her role as a homemaker and servant.

Patron Saint Of

cooks, dietitians, homemakers, servants
