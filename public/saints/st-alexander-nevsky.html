St. Alexander Nevsky

Feast Day: November 23
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Alexander was a brilliant general who defeated the Swedes on the Neva River and the Teutonic Knights on Lake Peipus. He used his diplomatic skills to manage the relationship with the Golden Horde, saving Russia from complete destruction. He took monastic vows on his deathbed.

Did you know? He was voted the 'Greatest Russian' in a nationwide 2008 TV poll.

Patron Saint Of

Russia, soldiers, St. Petersburg
