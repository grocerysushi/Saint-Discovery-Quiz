St. Francis Caracciolo

Feast Day: June 4

Biography

St. Francis Caracciolo dedicated his life to serving God through the Eucharist and showed a profound love for the Blessed Sacrament. He founded a religious order focused on the love and adoration of the Eucharist, emphasizing the importance of this sacrament in daily life. His deep spirituality and commitment to service inspired many to follow his example of faith and devotion.

Did you know? St. Francis was known to perform miracles attributed to the power of the Eucharist, including healing illnesses.
