Biography

St. Christopher is known as the patron saint of travelers, embodying the spirit of adventure and protection. According to tradition, he carried a child across a perilous river, only to discover that the child was Christ himself. His unwavering faith and sacrificial love ultimately led to his martyrdom.

Did you know? St. Christopher's name means 'Christ-bearer,' which reflects his famous act of carrying Jesus.

Patron Saint Of
