Biography

St. Therese of Lisieux, also known as the Little Flower, entered the Carmelite convent at a young age and dedicated her life to God through small acts of love. Her spiritual writings, especially her autobiography 'Story of a Soul,' have inspired countless individuals to embrace simplicity in their spiritual lives. Despite her short life, she was declared a Doctor of the Church and remains one of the most beloved saints in Catholicism.

Did you know? St. Therese reportedly said that after her death, she would let fall a shower of roses from heaven as a sign of her intercession.
