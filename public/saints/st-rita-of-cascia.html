St. Rita of Cascia

Feast Day: May 22
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Rita married a cruel man against her will and endured 18 years of abuse before his murder. After her husband and two sons died, she entered an Augustinian convent. She is famous for a wound on her forehead that resembled a thorn from Christ's crown which she bore for 15 years.

Did you know? On her deathbed, she asked for a rose from her garden in mid-winter, and a single blooming rose was miraculously found.
