Biography

St. Elizabeth Ann Seton was a devoted wife and mother who faced personal tragedies, including the loss of her husband. She responded to her call to serve God by founding the first Catholic school in the United States and established the Sisters of Charity. Her legacy continues in the mission of education and charitable service throughout the country.

Did you know? St. Elizabeth Ann Seton opened the first free Catholic school in the United States in 1809.
