Biography

St. Adrian of Nicomedia was a Christian martyr who lived during the early 4th century. He boldly refused to renounce his faith, even when faced with brutal torture and death. His steadfastness in the face of adversity has inspired countless believers throughout the ages.

Did you know? St. Adrian is often depicted with a sword, symbolizing his role as a protector of Christians.

Patron Saint Of

guards, soldiers, and those in danger.

Life & Origin

Feast Day: September 11

Lived: d. 304

Origin: Nicomedia, Asia Minor
