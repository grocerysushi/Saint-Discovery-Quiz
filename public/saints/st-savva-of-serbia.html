St. Savva of Serbia

Feast Day: January 14
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Savva was a prince who secretly fled to Mount Athos to become a monk. He later returned to Serbia to establish its independent church, founded schools and hospitals, and negotiated peace between his brothers. He is considered the father of Serbian culture and identity.

Did you know? He is credited with bringing 'European' education and law to medieval Serbia.
