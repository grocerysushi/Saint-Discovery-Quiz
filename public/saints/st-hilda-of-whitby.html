St. Hilda of Whitby

Feast Day: November 17
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Hilda was a noblewoman of the kingdom of Deira. She was a powerful and wise administrator who trained five future bishops at her monastery. She hosted the historic Synod of Whitby, which decided how the English Church would calculate the date of Easter.

Did you know? She is the patroness of Caedmon, the first known English poet, whom she encouraged to write after hearing of his miraculous gift from an angel.
