St. Casimir

Biography

St. Casimir was a Polish prince who dedicated his life to serving God and leading a life of virtue. Known for his deep faith, he refused to succumb to worldly temptations and was a model of Christian charity. His legacy continues to inspire the youth of Poland and Lithuania to strive for purity and devotion.

Did you know? St. Casimir is often depicted in art wearing princely robes and a crown, but holding a lily, symbolizing his purity.
