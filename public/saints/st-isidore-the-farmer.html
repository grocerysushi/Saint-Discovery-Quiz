Biography

St. Isidore the Farmer is revered for his deep faith and connection to the land, embodying the spirit of Christian labor. He is often depicted surrounded by animals, reflecting his kindness and stewardship over creation. His commitment to daily Mass and charity towards the poor made him a beloved figure in his community.

Did you know? St. Isidore is said to have experienced miraculous interventions, with angels assisting him in his farming work.
