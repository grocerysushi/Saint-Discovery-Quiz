Biography

St. Willibald was an English monk who set out on a pilgrimage to the Holy Land. His travels took him across Europe, where he shared the Christian faith and established monasteries. His dedication to spreading the Gospel and his commitment to the monastic life made him a revered figure in early Christianity.
