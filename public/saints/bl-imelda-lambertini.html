Bl. Imelda Lambertini

Feast Day: May 13
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

Blessed Imelda Lambertini was a young Italian girl devoted to the Eucharist, who yearned to receive Holy Communion. She experienced a profound encounter with Jesus in the Blessed Sacrament, which led to her dying in a state of joyful ecstasy. Her life exemplifies deep faith and innocence, inspiring many in the Catholic Church.

Did you know? Imelda is often depicted in art holding a host or standing in a beam of light, symbolizing her deep devotion to the Eucharist.
