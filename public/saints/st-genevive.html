Biography

St. Geneviève was a devout young woman from Nanterre, France, who dedicated her life to God. She is best remembered for her intercessory prayers that helped protect Paris from barbarian invasions in the 5th century. Despite facing significant opposition, her unwavering faith and leadership inspired many in the community to trust in divine protection during perilous times.

Did you know? St. Geneviève was known to have a vision of St. Denis, which affirmed her calling to protect Paris.
