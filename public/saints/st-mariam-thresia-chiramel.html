Biography

St. Mariam Thresia Chiramel Mankidiyan was an Indian mystic of the Syro-Malabar Church who experienced extraordinary spiritual phenomena from childhood. She founded the Congregation of the Holy Family to serve broken families, the sick, and the dying. Despite facing misunderstanding and opposition, she persevered in her mission.

Did you know? She was initially accused of being possessed by demons due to her mystical experiences, but was eventually vindicated by Church authorities.
