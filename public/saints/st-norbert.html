St. Norbert

Feast Day: June 6

Biography

St. Norbert was born around 1080 in Xanten, Germany, and experienced a profound conversion after a near-death experience. Committed to reforming the clergy and promoting a communal monastic life, he founded the Premonstratensian order, emphasizing discipline and spiritual integrity. He played a pivotal role in advocating for peace during his lifetime and continued to inspire generations through his dedication to God and the Church.

Did you know? St. Norbert is said to have once survived a dramatic encounter with a stag, which greatly influenced his understanding of divine intervention.

Patron Saint Of

Bohemia, expectant mothers, peace, advocates of peace, victims of abuse
