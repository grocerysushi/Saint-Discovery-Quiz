St. Augustine Zhao Rong

Feast Day: July 9

Biography

St. Augustine Zhao Rong was a Chinese soldier who guarded a bishop being transported to execution. So moved by the bishop's courage, he converted to Christianity and eventually became a diocesan priest. He was arrested and tortured to death during the Qing Dynasty's persecution of Christians.

Did you know? He converted after escorting a condemned bishop to his execution—watching the man's faith and courage transformed his own life completely.
