St. Lawrence

Feast Day: August 10
Home Take Quiz All Saints By Virtue By Century Feast Calendar

Biography

St. Lawrence was a devoted deacon in Rome who became known for his unwavering faith and service to the poor. He bravely faced his martyrdom around the year 258, famously being roasted alive on a gridiron while expressing his unshakeable faith. His legacy continues to inspire acts of charity and comedic humility in the face of adversity.

Did you know? St. Lawrence is often depicted with a gridiron and was known for humorously telling his executioners to turn him over because he was done on one side.
