Biography

St. Benjamin was a deacon who courageously preached Christianity during a time of persecution. He was captured and subsequently martyred for his faith, serving as an inspiration to many. His steadfast commitment to his beliefs continues to resonate with those who advocate for social justice and human rights.

Did you know? St. Benjamin is often portrayed with a scroll, representing his role in spreading the message of the Gospel.
